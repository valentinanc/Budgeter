About Us

Budgeter is a free website that lets users keep track of a budget and their financial goals. With Budgeter, you will be able to customize your budget by adding new categories for expenses and savings. Budgeter also provides visualizations to help you see your short run and long run progress with keeping up with a budget. We do this by tracking your average expenses, savings, and how much of your budget you fulfill every month, using the information to display it on graphs. With Budgeter, you will be more aware of where your money is going.

Our Goals

We hope that by using Budgeter, users will be more aware of their spending habits, and will be able to budget accordingly to meet their financial goals. In particular, we hope our users will be able to:

  • Create a personalized budget with categories that they can choose or add.
  • Visualize their savings, budgets, and expenses in form of graphs and tables.
  • Be reminded of their financial goals.
  • Be notified about when they exceed their budget.

Key Features




Financial Goals

Budgeter lets users add financial goals, motivating them to keep up with their budget.

Budget Customization

Budgeter provides users with the ability to add niche categories that fit their spending habits.

Dashboard Visualizations

Budgeter provides detailed graphs to help users visualize how they are spending their money.
